Transfer function approach (轉移函數方法) State-space approach (狀態空間方法)
描述 頻域，經典做法 時域，現代方法

    yj(t)=gj[x1(t),x2(t),......,xn(t),u1(t),u2(t),......,um(t)]y_j(t) = g_j[x_1(t), x_2(t), ......, x_n(t), u_1(t), u_2(t), ......, u_m(t)]yj​(t)=gj​[x1​(t),x2​(t),......,xn​(t),u1​(t),u2​(t),......,um​(t)]
